About Grand City

Grand City is a shopping center that was established in 2000. Grand City is located in West Jakarta, Indonesia. It is one of the largest shopping centers in Indonesia with more than 10,000 visitors every day.

Our Shop

Grand City offers an International shopping experience and a wide selection of fashion, restaurants, and entertainment. There are several well-known international fashion and lifestyle brands for all ages in Grand City such as Pull & Bear, Levi's, Mothercare, ELC, Justice, Dr. Marten., Nike, Adidas, etc.

Service & Facilities

Grand City provides facilities such as toilets, nursery rooms, children's play areas, disabled parking, elevators, and atm centers. We always maintain cleanliness so that customers feel comfortable using it. In addition, we also provide an open garden for customers to relax and enjoy the view.
